PROJECT OVERVIEW

After the outstanding success of Alphathum, Bhutani Infra is presenting Grandthum, a new landmark commercial project in Greater Noida West. The project will include extraordinary designed retail spaces, office spaces, serviced apartments, and much more under one flexible ecosystem.

Retail and office spaces at Grandthum are available in varied sizes to match the needs and budgets of investors. If you're looking out to make a safe commercial real estate investment, you're at the right place.

  • Integrated with Yatch Island – an ultra-modern Amusement Park
  • Houses Yatch Garden – a luxurious and elegant dining space
  • A mixed-use development spreading over 25 acres
